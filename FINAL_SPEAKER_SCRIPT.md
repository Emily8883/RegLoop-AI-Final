# RegLoop AI Final Speaker Script

Use this as a short narration track for the final demo.

## Opening

"RegLoop AI is a closed-loop regulatory execution prototype that turns a regulation into obligations, gaps, policy recommendations, human review actions, audit evidence, and exportable output."

## Documents

"This workspace collects one regulation, one to three policy documents, and a responsibility matrix in one place."

"For the demo, the challenge sample bundle is already loaded so we can focus on the workflow outcome."

## Obligations

"The platform extracts structured obligations from the regulation and stores confidence scores and source citations."

## Compliance

"The compliance page creates gap records and shows which obligations need attention first."

## Policy Review

"Policy recommendations are proposed as reviewable pull requests. AI suggests changes, but humans stay in control and approve, reject, modify, or escalate each one."

## Audit Trail

"Every meaningful step is recorded in the audit trail, so we can trace a document from upload through final review."

## Export

"The final compliance package can be exported in JSON or CSV for reporting and submission use."

## Closing

"That gives the team a guided compliance workflow instead of a manual, disconnected review process."
